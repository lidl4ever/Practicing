Our Team

This project is a collaboration of two teams: Charles Huang, the co-creator of the Guitar Hero® video game franchise, led Guitar Hero to become the 2nd video game franchise in history to reach $1B USD, a mark he achieved 3 times. He also co-founded Green Throttle, an Android game controller platform acquired by Google. At Indigo 7 Silicon Valley, he and his team of successful entrepreneurs and video game industry veterans have held executive roles at industry giants including Activision®, SNK®, and Namco®.

Sean Moss-Pultz and his Bitmark team (blockchain startup invested by Cherubic, DCG, WI Harper, Alibaba) provide the technical blockchain knowledge. Bitmark currently works with organizations including the University of California at Berkeley and KKBox (streaming music service, the Spotify of Asia with over 10M paid subscribers in Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Singapore) in deploying its property title blockchain technology.

Together, Sean and Charles lead a team of 20 in the Silicon Valley (Cupertino, California), Taiwan, and Vietnam.
