Utility Token


The GAMEngine Utility Token, or “GME”,
grants access to the platform.



TOKENS FOR ADMISSION

Tokens are not tied directly to any revenue streams, bitmarks or bounties but act as a community gateway. The number of tokens that a community member holds will dictate the level of economic opportunity available to them within GAMEngine. These tokens ensure that any participant, whether passive or active, is a true stakeholder in the platform. DECENTRALIZED GOVERNANCE


GAMEngine is a DAO, or Decentralized Autonomous Organization, that is not owned or controlled by any centralized entity. The platform is a balanced, sustainable ecosystem governed by its token holders. This ensures that no single party gains an advantage over another because all parties have the power to influence how it operates. Together, Creators, Investors, and Fans decide on how GAMEngine grows and develops in order to maximize success for all stakeholders.

voting rights


Token holders can propose and vote on potential improvements to the platform. These improvements can include things like process changes (e.g. submission requirements), economic changes (e.g. token fees) or fee restructuring. Changes in the platform and additions to the ecosystem will be funded by the operational pool.
